All about Stationery

What is Stationery?

A connection between your hand and brain!

  • To put it simple, it’s the materials we use to write! Pens, pencils, markers, etc, but it also includes different materials you use when writing like paper, rulers, and even stickers!

  • My interest in stationery is he individuality that comes with it. Everyone has their favourite supplies that are unique to the person! Do you prefer to write with gel pens? Brush pens? Ball point? Thick? Thin? I think its interesting to learn about someone through stationery as well. You can connect with people!

  • I think it’s important to have good stationery supplies to help you out your ideas on paper. I find It helps my ideas flow better when I’m writing with good supplies. The pen doesn’t get in my way of thinking and I can write freely. It also just feels really nice and satisfying to have good supplies.
